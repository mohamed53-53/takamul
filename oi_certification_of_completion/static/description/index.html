Certification Of Completion

"Easily Manage Progress Payments"

This module helps you to make payments in different stages and create related accounting entries.

Description

When it comes to purchasing a service, the vendor may deliver your orders in different phases so that the payments can be executed in several stages.

Certificate of Completion is a document issued by the client, which indicates that the service or portion of it has been delivered at the specific phase of the work in all respects as per the specifications and conditions of the contract. This allows clients to pay their vendors incrementally as the project is in progress.

“COC” module allows you to make payments at each phase of the delivery in accordance with the part of the service provided and issue the related vendor bill and also enables you to post the related journal entries. It also calculates the remaining amount of the bill and keeps it open until the delivery of the service is completed.
